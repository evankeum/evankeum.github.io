Experiences
Undergraduate Researcher
I was part of the Undergraduate Research Apprentice Program where I helped carefully classify the different orders of arthropods within a cluster containing up to 25 different almost "microscopic" arthropods using a microscope and a handbook. I had to differentiate them according to the number of limbs, and other body structures. The goal of the research was to develop metrics of diversity and abundance over extended time and provide insights into biodiversity assembly dynamics to the different Hawaiian arthropods over geological chronosequence.
